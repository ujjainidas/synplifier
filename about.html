    • About Us

    We are Synplifier, a free and accessible tool created for healthcare professionals worldwide! Our web application allows scientists to translate production protocols into robust and user friendly walkthroughs.

    We have created a compiler for synthetic biology, from an academic lab protocol to standardized commands to a step-by-step breakdown.

    Essential protocols in synthetic biology, growing, transforming, and inducing bacteria, can be reduced to:

    • The transfer of liquids.
    • Periods of raised and lowered temperature.
